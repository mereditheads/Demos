Chef Name

Stephen Harman is the newest addition to The Coastal Cupboard staff and he is welcomed with open arms as our in-house chef. He arrives with a varied culinary background, including: sous chef at the Mustard Seed, experiences throughout Europe in both food and wine, exposure to corporate cuisine in Chicago at J. Alexander’s and Weber Grill, and with his return to Charleston in 2008, executive chef of Hucks Lowcountry Table...

He brings with him a passion for cooking with fresh local produce, preference for flavoring dishes with herbs straight from his garden, and the desire to teach healthy, delicious, and cost effective recipes for toddlers to adults. When not working, Stephen enjoys spending time with his wife, Ruthie and his young son, Taver…word has it that Taver loves to help his dad in the garden and with the fresh herbs…from the dirt up! Stephen also likes to play golf and relax at the beach.

The Experience

Here is a snapshot of what to expect when you come spend an evening with us in one of the Coastal Cupboard cooking classes: Our cooking class guests arrive a bit before 6:30 when the classes begin, so immediately our guests have the whole store to themselves.

This allows for a relaxing, quiet, and distraction-free environment where guests can sit and dine while interacting in an intimate setting with the chef and other cooking class guests. Throughout the evening the class is served anywhere from 3-5 courses, which are prepared, plated, and served like you were at one of the excellent restaurants these chefs represent. The meal is accompanied by wines, beers, coffee and/or infused waters to compliment the incredible food. Most classes run about 2-3 hours in length, with a brief intermission where the store is open exclusively for the cooking class guests to shop at their leisure. We also offer discount incentives, just in case you need the excuse in justifying another silly gadget that the chef swears you can’t cook without! The ultimate goal for us is to create a fun, educational, unique, and memorable experience. So whether you’re a cook, a foodie, or someone who just wants a great night out with friends or your special someone – please give us a try to provide you with an evening that will last in your recipe box for years.
